What this Asia quiz covers

Asia stretches from the Middle East and Central Asia to India, China, Southeast Asia, and the Pacific coast. The map combines some of the world's largest countries with dense clusters of smaller neighbors.

How the point map quiz works

A point appears somewhere inside one of the places in this group. Type the name of the country that contains it. The round covers all 51 countries and tests whether you understand the full area of each place rather than only recognizing its outline.

Gameplay tip: Check the point against coastlines and nearby countries before answering, especially when it sits close to a border.

What makes this group challenging

Central Asia, the Caucasus, and mainland Southeast Asia contain several neighboring countries that are commonly confused. The continent also stretches across a very wide map.

Study tip

Study Asia by subregion before attempting the full quiz, especially South Asia, Central Asia, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East.
