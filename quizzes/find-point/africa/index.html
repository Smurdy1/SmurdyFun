What this Africa quiz covers

Africa contains more than fifty answers in this group, including North Africa, West Africa, the Horn, central Africa, southern Africa, and nearby island countries. It is one of the site's most detailed continent quizzes.

How the point map quiz works

A point appears somewhere inside one of the places in this group. Type the name of the country that contains it. The round covers all 56 countries and tests whether you understand the full area of each place rather than only recognizing its outline.

Gameplay tip: Check the point against coastlines and nearby countries before answering, especially when it sits close to a border.

What makes this group challenging

West Africa contains many smaller countries with short coastlines, while central and eastern Africa have several large inland neighbors that can blend together at first.

Study tip

Learn the coastline first, then use those coastal countries to place the landlocked countries behind them.
