What this Caribbean Islands quiz covers

The Caribbean group focuses on sovereign countries spread across the Caribbean Sea and nearby Atlantic waters. It includes the large islands of the Greater Antilles and the smaller independent states curving toward South America.

How the typing map quiz works

One country is highlighted on the map. Type its name to answer, using the map shape and location as your clue. The round continues through all 13 countries, making this useful for spelling practice as well as map recognition.

Gameplay tip: Say each answer in your head before typing it. That small pause helps connect the written name to the map.

What makes this group challenging

Several countries occupy small islands that appear close together, and some neighboring islands are territories rather than independent countries.

Study tip

Start with Cuba, Hispaniola, Jamaica, and Puerto Rico as landmarks, then trace the smaller independent countries toward Trinidad and Tobago.
