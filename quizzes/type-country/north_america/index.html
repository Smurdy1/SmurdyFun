What this North America quiz covers

North America in this quiz includes Canada, the United States, Mexico, Central America, and the sovereign Caribbean countries stored in the group. Three very large mainland countries share the map with many smaller tropical states.

How the typing map quiz works

One country is highlighted on the map. Type its name to answer, using the map shape and location as your clue. The round continues through all 23 countries, making this useful for spelling practice as well as map recognition.

Gameplay tip: Say each answer in your head before typing it. That small pause helps connect the written name to the map.

What makes this group challenging

The Caribbean islands and the narrow Central American isthmus require closer map reading than Canada, the United States, or Mexico.

Study tip

Master the mainland from north to south, then learn the Caribbean as a separate cluster before combining them.
