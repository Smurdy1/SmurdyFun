What this South America quiz covers

South America has a manageable number of countries and several clear coastal shapes. The group runs from Colombia and Venezuela in the north to Argentina and Chile in the south.

How the typing map quiz works

One country is highlighted on the map. Type its name to answer, using the map shape and location as your clue. The round continues through all 12 countries, making this useful for spelling practice as well as map recognition.

Gameplay tip: Say each answer in your head before typing it. That small pause helps connect the written name to the map.

What makes this group challenging

The Guianas in the northeast and the inland positions of Bolivia and Paraguay are the places most likely to cause mistakes.

Study tip

Use Brazil as the central anchor, then learn which countries touch its long border and which do not.
