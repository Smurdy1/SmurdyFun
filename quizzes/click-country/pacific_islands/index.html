What this Pacific Islands quiz covers

Pacific Islands focuses on the independent countries scattered across the Pacific, including Melanesian, Micronesian, and Polynesian states in Smurdy's group list. Australia and New Zealand are left out so the island nations remain the focus.

How the click map quiz works

The quiz gives you one country at a time. Click the matching area on the map, then continue until you have completed all 12 countries. Borders remain visible, so this mode works well for first learning the shapes and positions.

Gameplay tip: Start with the largest or most familiar places, then use their borders to place nearby answers.

What makes this group challenging

The countries are separated by huge ocean distances, and several are represented by very small map areas. Relative position is more useful than border shape.

Study tip

Use Papua New Guinea and Fiji as reference points, then learn which countries lie north, east, or northeast of them.
