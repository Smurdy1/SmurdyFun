What this Latin America quiz covers

Latin America connects Mexico, Central America, much of the Caribbean, and South America in one broad regional set. The grouping mainly follows shared Spanish, Portuguese, and French colonial language history.

How the click map quiz works

The quiz gives you one country at a time. Click the matching area on the map, then continue until you have completed all 21 countries. Borders remain visible, so this mode works well for first learning the shapes and positions.

Gameplay tip: Start with the largest or most familiar places, then use their borders to place nearby answers.

What makes this group challenging

The region stretches across two continents and includes both mainland countries and islands, so players must shift between several different map scales.

Study tip

Learn Central America and South America separately, then add the Caribbean countries included in the group.
