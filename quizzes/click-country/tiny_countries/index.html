What this Tiny Countries quiz covers

Tiny Countries gathers small sovereign states from Europe, the Caribbean, the Indian Ocean, and the Pacific. These are the places most likely to disappear into a normal world map at first glance.

How the click map quiz works

The quiz gives you one country at a time. Click the matching area on the map, then continue until you have completed all 28 countries. Borders remain visible, so this mode works well for first learning the shapes and positions.

Gameplay tip: Start with the largest or most familiar places, then use their borders to place nearby answers.

What makes this group challenging

Most answers occupy very little screen space, and many do not share land borders with larger countries. Precise zooming and recognition of regional context matter more than outline shape.

Study tip

Group the countries by region, especially European microstates, Caribbean islands, and Pacific island nations.
