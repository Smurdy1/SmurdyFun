What this South and Central Asia quiz covers

South and Central Asia links the Indian subcontinent with the landlocked countries between the Caspian Sea, China, Russia, and Afghanistan. The combined set shows how the two neighboring regions fit together.

How the click map quiz works

The quiz gives you one country at a time. Click the matching area on the map, then continue until you have completed all 13 countries. Borders remain visible, so this mode works well for first learning the shapes and positions.

Gameplay tip: Start with the largest or most familiar places, then use their borders to place nearby answers.

What makes this group challenging

The Central Asian countries have similar names and broad inland borders, while South Asia has several smaller states around India.

Study tip

Use India and Kazakhstan as the two main anchors, then learn the countries surrounding each one.
