What this Central America and the Caribbean quiz covers

Central America and the Caribbean combines the seven mainland countries between Mexico and Colombia with the sovereign island countries of the Caribbean. It gives focused practice for places that are easy to lose inside a full North America round.

How the click map quiz works

The quiz gives you one country at a time. Click the matching area on the map, then continue until you have completed all 20 countries. Borders remain visible, so this mode works well for first learning the shapes and positions.

Gameplay tip: Start with the largest or most familiar places, then use their borders to place nearby answers.

What makes this group challenging

Central America forms a narrow chain with several similarly sized neighbors, while the Caribbean requires remembering the order and position of separate islands.

Study tip

Learn Central America north to south, then study the Greater Antilles and Lesser Antilles as separate island chains.
